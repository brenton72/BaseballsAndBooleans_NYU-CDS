
When we were indexing the pitch type, we use:

	1 for FF
	2 for SL
	3 for FT
	4 for CU
	5 for CH
	6 for SI
	7 for FC
	8 for KC
	9 for NaN
	0 for other types
	

When we were indexing the pitch result, we use:

	1 for B
	2 for X
	3 for C
	4 for F
	0 for S


When we were indexing the pitcher/batter's hand, we use:

	1 for R
	0 for L
